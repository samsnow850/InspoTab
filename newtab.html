About InspoTab

InspoTab is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab page with daily motivational quotes and beautiful backgrounds.

Every time you open a new tab, you'll see a random inspirational quote to keep you motivated throughout your day.

Created by: Samuel Snow

Website: inspo.samuelesnow.co
